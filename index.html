In Missile Commander (in hommage to Missile Command) you play as a Human Commander trying to win back the 1337 galaxy sectors that have fallen into the hands of the Enemy.

Test your stress levels and shoot those aliens down!

Developped by HeyHumans! (@HeyHumansGames) with music and sounds by Matias Lizana García (@matias_lizana) and visuals by Christian Ung (@christianung).

We opensourced all the game assets, they can be found here:

  • main repo (game, sound, visuals): https://github.com/HeyHumansGames/missile-commander
  • sound on Matias' bandcamp page
  • visuals on OpenGameArt: http://opengameart.org/content/missile-commander-pixel-art-space-sprites

We hope you like it and help us win back the galaxy!
